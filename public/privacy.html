Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

What DailyDuel stores

DailyDuel is a browser word game. Your game statistics (wins, streaks, XP, settings) are stored only in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is sent to our servers. We have no accounts, no logins, and we do not collect names, emails, or any personal information.

Advertising

We may display ads served by Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies or device identifiers to show ads based on your visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings. See how Google uses data from partner sites.

Analytics

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics, which is cookie-free and does not track you across sites or collect personal data.
