About DailyDuel

A free, head-to-head take on the daily word puzzle.

What DailyDuel is

DailyDuel is a free browser word game built around one simple idea: solving the daily puzzle is more fun when it is a race. Every day there is a single five-letter word, the same for everyone, and your goal is to find it in six guesses. The twist is that you can play it head to head — against a smart bot or against a friend in real time — with both boards side by side and the crown going to whoever solves first.

Why we made it

Solo daily word games are great, but they end the moment you finish. DailyDuel keeps the same satisfying daily puzzle and adds the thing that makes games sticky: another person to beat. Racing a friend to the same word turns a quiet two-minute solve into a rivalry, and the shareable result — "I solved it in 4.2 seconds, can you beat me?" — is half the fun. It runs entirely in the browser, so there is nothing to install and nothing to sign up for.

What is inside

  • A daily five-letter puzzle that is identical for every player and resets at midnight.
  • Practice mode for a relaxed solo solve with no pressure.
  • A smart bot opponent that plays with real word-solving strategy.
  • Live friend duels over a shared room link, racing the same word in real time.
  • Asynchronous friend challenges that carry your solve time for a friend to beat.
  • XP, levels, ranks from Bronze to Crown, daily streaks, and detailed stats.
  • Four color themes, sound effects, and a mobile-friendly layout.

Fair play and privacy

In a live duel you can see your opponent's tile colors but never their letters, so there is no way to read the answer off their board. DailyDuel has no accounts and keeps your stats, streaks, and settings only in your browser's local storage — nothing is uploaded to a server. See the privacy policy for the full detail on analytics and advertising.

How it stays free

DailyDuel is kept free with unobtrusive advertising. The core game — daily puzzle, practice, bot duels, and friend duels — is fully playable at no cost, and there is nothing you need to buy to enjoy it.
